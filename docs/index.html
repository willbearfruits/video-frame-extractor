Video to Photogrammetry

A simple, privacy-focused tool to extract high-quality, sharp frames from video for 3D reconstruction. Runs 100% locally on your machine.

Download Tool (Zip) View Source

How to Use

1

Download & Extract

Download the zip file. Right-click and select "Extract All" to unzip it on your computer.

2

Run the Installer

Double-click install_and_run.bat. It will automatically set up Python and open the tool.

3

Drag & Extract

Drag your video into the browser window. Choose your settings and click Start. Done!

Why not a web app?

Video processing for photogrammetry requires heavy computation (blur detection, high-res extraction). Running this locally ensures it works fast, handles large files (4K/8K), and keeps your data private.
